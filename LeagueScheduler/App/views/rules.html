League Rules

Standard High School rules will be used with the following additions:

  • 28.5" ball will be used (29.5" is used starting in 7th grade)
  • Four 10-minute quarters with running clock. Clock stops on time-outs and in last minute of each quarter.
  • Each team gets 2 time-outs per game (they do not carry forward to overtime)
  • Two Free throws are awarded on 10th team foul of each half. No one-and-one's.
  • No press rule when team is winning by 15 or more
  • Scorebook will be kept for player fouls - players foul out at 5 fouls.
  • For each game, one team should provide a volunteer to work the scoreboard and the other to keep the scorebook.
  • If replacement players are used (for teams short on players), the replacements must be registered HYBA players from 6th grade division.
